Frequently Asked Questions

What makes EnergyBites different?

We use only whole food ingredients with no artificial additives, preservatives, or refined sugars. Each bar is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and freshness.

Are EnergyBites gluten-free?

Yes! All our bars are naturally gluten-free. We process them in a dedicated gluten-free facility to prevent cross-contamination.

How long do the bars stay fresh?

Our bars stay fresh for 6 months when stored at room temperature. For best results, store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship to the US and Canada. Visit our shipping page for more details.

Are your bars vegan?

Our Fruit Fusion and Seed Supreme bars are 100% vegan. The Nuts & Honey bar contains honey and is suitable for vegetarians.
