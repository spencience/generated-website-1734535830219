Our Story

From Kitchen to Community

Founded in 2018 by health-conscious food enthusiasts Sarah and Mike Thompson, EnergyBites began in a small kitchen in Portland, Oregon. What started as a quest for the perfect hiking snack has grown into a mission to provide wholesome, natural energy bars to active individuals worldwide.

Every EnergyBites bar is crafted with care using sustainably sourced ingredients, supporting local farmers and organic practices whenever possible.
